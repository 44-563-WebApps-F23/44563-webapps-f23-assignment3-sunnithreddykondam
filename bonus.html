Karl Scheduler

Karl is a smart professor who works at a remotely located village known as Maryville. He was one of the first immigrant professors at the University. His technical depth on the subject and humourous teaching style made him the best faculty at the University.


Interesting physical/personality traits of Karl:

  • Although his name has a Scheduler as last name, he always been late on time.
  • He is an staunch supporter of Socialistic ideology just like Karl Marx.
  • Although he eats a lot, he has a good physique.
